📢 Math Olympiad 2025 - Competition Details

🏆 Eligibility Criteria

The Math Olympiad is open to elementary school students in **Grades 1 to 5** from all over the world.

📌 Age Group

  • Grade 1: 5-6 years
  • Grade 2: 6-7 years
  • Grade 3: 7-8 years
  • Grade 4: 8-9 years
  • Grade 5: 9-10 years

🎁 Prizes & Rewards

  • 🏅 **Gold Medal & Certificate** for 1st place in each grade
  • 🥈 **Silver Medal & Certificate** for 2nd place in each grade
  • 🥉 **Bronze Medal & Certificate** for 3rd place in each grade
  • 🎗️ **Participation Certificates** for all students

📊 Difficulty Level

The questions are divided based on grade level:

  • Grade 1-2: Basic arithmetic, patterns, simple word problems
  • Grade 3-4: Multiplication, division, fractions, logical puzzles
  • Grade 5: Advanced arithmetic, geometry basics, algebraic thinking

📜 Rules & Guidelines

  • ⏳ The competition is **60 minutes long**.
  • 📝 Students must answer **20 multiple-choice questions**.
  • 🚫 **No calculators** or external assistance is allowed.
  • 🎯 The **top 3 students per grade** will be awarded medals.
